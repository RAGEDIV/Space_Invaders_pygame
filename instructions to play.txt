 to play the game all the controls are in key board:
	for player movement:
	(i) w or up arrow for upward movement
	(ii) d or right arrow for right side movement
	(iii) s or down arrow for down movement
	(iv) a or  left arrow key for let side movement
	(v) space bar to shoot bullet
